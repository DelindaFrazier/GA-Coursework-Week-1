About Me

I enjoy making good content look great.

For the last 13 years I've worked as a events and technology specialist for a nonprofit association where I was able to nurture my love of technology and digital media by managing the technologies used to support our meetings (Web, mobile, database, audio visual). I enjoyed the fast-paced, deadline driven environment of meeting planning and especially enjoyed working across the association with different departments to create digital content for our meetings.

I have experience developing and managing web content with content management systems (SharePoint, Kentico, WordPress, WebCenter Sites), web development (Dreamweaver, HTML, CSS), social media and writing for the web.

In May 2015 I earned a Masters of Arts in Interactive Journalism from American University. Through coursework, I acquired skills in photography, video and audio recording and editing(Adobe CC Audition and Premiere Pro) and exposure to data visualization, media law and ethics.
